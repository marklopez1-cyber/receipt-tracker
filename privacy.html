🧾 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

ReceiptTrack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our expense management application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address and password (encrypted) when you create an account
  • Receipt data: Store names, dates, amounts, categories, payment types, and notes you enter or scan
  • Receipt images: Photos of receipts you upload for AI scanning (stored locally on your device only)
  • Usage data: Number of AI scans used per month for billing enforcement
  • Payment information: Handled entirely by Stripe — we never store credit card numbers
  • Team data: Organization name and member email addresses for Business plan users

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the ReceiptTrack service
  • To process AI-powered receipt scanning via Anthropic's Claude API
  • To send team invitations via email (Resend)
  • To process subscription payments (Stripe)
  • To enforce scan limits based on your subscription tier
  • To provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your receipt data is stored securely in Supabase, a PostgreSQL database with row-level security. Receipt images are stored only in your browser's local storage (IndexedDB) and are never uploaded to our servers. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication (supabase.com)
  • Anthropic — AI receipt scanning via Claude API (anthropic.com)
  • Stripe — Payment processing (stripe.com)
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery (resend.com)

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and receipt data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Receipt images stored locally in your browser can be cleared by clearing your browser data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data (available via the Backup & Restore feature)
  • Opt out of marketing communications

7. Children's Privacy

ReceiptTrack is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the app. Continued use of ReceiptTrack after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
